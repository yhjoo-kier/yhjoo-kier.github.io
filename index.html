About

Senior Researcher at the Korea Institute of Energy Research (KIER), Associate Professor at the University of Science & Technology (UST).

B.S., M.S., and Ph.D. in Mechanical Engineering from KAIST (2012, 2014, 2017). Previously Senior Researcher at KAERI (2018–2021) and postdoctoral researcher at KAIST (2017–2018).

View CV Publications Contact

Research Interests

  • Energy efficiency of manufacturing processes — battery manufacturing and other energy-intensive processes such as drying and casting; process modeling, equipment-level optimization, and life-cycle energy analysis.
  • AI agents for industrial energy operation — autonomous operation of industrial facilities and energy-consuming equipment to reduce energy use while preserving product quality.
  • Optimal design of thermal systems — topology optimization of heat sinks, heat exchangers, and thermal insulators under natural/forced convection and multi-physics conditions.
  • Machine learning for engineering design — deep-learned finite elements, graph-based convergence acceleration, and reinforcement learning for simulation and design workflows.
