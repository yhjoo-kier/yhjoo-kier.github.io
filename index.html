About

Senior Researcher at the Korea Institute of Energy Research (KIER), Associate Professor at the University of Science & Technology (UST).

B.S., M.S., and Ph.D. in Mechanical Engineering from KAIST (2012, 2014, 2017). Previously Senior Researcher at KAERI (2018–2021) and postdoctoral researcher at KAIST (2017–2018).

View CV Publications Contact

Research Interests

  • Optimal design of energy systems — topology optimization of heat sinks, heat exchangers, thermal insulators, and multi-physics thermal components.
  • AI-based operation of energy systems — deep-learned finite elements, graph-based convergence acceleration, and reinforcement learning for design and control.
  • Industrial energy systems — energy-efficient steel casting, drying processes, and manufacturing KPIs.
  • Heat transfer & thermal management — natural convection, phase-change materials, solar thermoelectric generators.
